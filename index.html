About

Patty Buns

Patty Pans opened in March 2021 following the brilliant idea of two young restaurateurs (Brian and Jessy) to bring the first completely vegetarian burger restaurant to the city. The project was an instant triumph and we are now considered the best vegetarian burger restaurant in Europe. The position in the colorful and youthful district of London (Shoreditch) certainly contributed to this amazing success. And it's not all! Besides veggie burgers we also serve organic local beer.
